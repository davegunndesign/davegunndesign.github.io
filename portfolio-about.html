© 2023 Dave Gunn

Dave Gunn is a visual designer based in New Haven, Connecticut. With his experiences in high-profile marketing campaigns, corporate rebranding, and social media; he specializes in branding, digital, and motion design.


His meticulous approach to design goes beyond aesthetics; he believes that every design decision should be purposeful and strategic. Through a detail-oriented process and in-depth research, he delves into the clients' objectives, immersing himself in their brand ethos to craft pieces that convey their story authentically. Through blending creativity with user-centered thinking, Dave believes design can leave a lasting impression, evoke emotions, and spark meaningful connection.
