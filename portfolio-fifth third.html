Fifth Third Daytona

As the official bank of the Daytona International Speedway and the Daytona 500, MKTG collaborated with FIfth Third bank on some campaign pieces for their new debit card. The campaign included some illustrations in ATM advertisements, print ads, and digital assets in which I animated. Both a Snapchat ad ran for the 2019 Daytona 500, as well as a geotag filter on the track for race day.
